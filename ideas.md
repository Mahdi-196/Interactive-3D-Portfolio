## Implementation Notes

### Easy to Add (3D Geometry)
- Coat rack, filing cabinet, phone, globe, chess set, safe
- These can be built with basic Three.js geometry

### Requires Textures/Images
- Movie posters, wanted posters, newspaper clippings, certificates
- Cork board, street maps, photos
- Book spines, rug patterns

### Advanced Effects
- Smoke/dust particles (particle systems)
- Rain on window (animated texture)
- Venetian blind shadows (custom lighting)
- Neon glow (emissive materials)
